    Preset Names

  • As editing preset names is not possible using TouchOSC, you may edit preset names for up to 99 common presets (each at a time) on Manage Preset Names page. Only 20 presets are shown at a time. Navigate with Previous/Next buttons or by 0 / 20 / 40 / 60 / 80 (goto) buttons.

    Preset names may only have 15 characters or less. Exceeding characters are truncated. Enter name for desired preset and click adjacent Submit button.
